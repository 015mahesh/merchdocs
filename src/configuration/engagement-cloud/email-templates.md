## Email Templates

![]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link images/images/config-engagement-cloud-email-templates.png %}){: .zoom}
_Email Templates_

|Field|[Scope]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link configuration/scope.md %})|Description|
|--- |--- |--- |
|**Customer**|||
|New Account|Store View|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a "welcome" email to customers who open a new account.|
|New Account Confirmation Key|Store View|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a confirmation email when customers open a new account.|
|New Account Confirmation|Store View|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a confirmation email to customers who open a new account.|
|Forgot Password|Store View|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends an email to customers who forget their passwords.|
|Remind Password|Store View|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a password reminder email to customers who have forgotten their passwords.|
|Wish List Sharing|Store View|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a confirmation email when the customer shares a wish list.|
|Forgot Admin Password|Store View|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a password reminder email to Admin users who have forgotten their passwords.|
|**Newsletter**|||
|Success Email Template|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a confirmation email at the completion of a subscription request.|
|Subscription Confirmation|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a confirmation email when a customer subscribes.
|Unsubscribe Success|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a confirmation email to customers who have been unsubscribed by request.
|**Sales**|||
|New Order Confirmation|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a confirmation email when a customer submits an order.|
|New Order Confirmation for Guest|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends a confirmation email when a guest submits an order.|
|Order Update|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies customers when their orders are updated.|
|Order Update for Guest|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies guests when their orders are updated.|
|New Shipment|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies customers when their orders ship.|
|New Shipment for Guest|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies guests when their orders ship.|
|Shipment Update|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies customers of updates to their shipments.|
|New Invoice|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies customers when a new invoice is generated.|
|New Invoice Guest|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies guests when a new invoice is generated.|
|Invoice Update|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies customers when an invoice is updated.|
|Invoice Update Guest|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies guests when an invoice is updated.|
|New Credit Credit Memo|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies customers when a credit memo is generated.|
|New Credit Credit Memo Guest|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies guests when a credit memo is generated.|
|Credit Memo Update|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies customers when a credit memo is updated.|
|Credit Memo Update Guest|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that notifies guests when a credit memo is updated.|
|**Contact**|||
|New Contact Form|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends confirmation email when a new contact record is submitted.|
|**Email**|||
|Send Product to Friend|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends notification when a customer shares a product to a friend.|
|**Product Alerts**|||
|Stock Alert|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends notification to a customer of a stock alert.|
|Product Price Alert|Website|Specifies the name of the campaign that sends notification to a customer of a product price alert.|
